Interesting and relevant facts:

  1. The wolverine is a powerful animal that resembles a small bear but is actually the largest member of the Mustelidae (weasel) family.
  2. Their habitats include Alpine forests, boreal forests, grasslands, tundra and rocky areas (inland cliffs and mountain peaks).
  3. Wolverines have thick, dark, oily fur which is highly hydrophobic, making it resistant to frost making them experts at living in the Arctic.
  4. Wolverines have poor eyesight, but their senses of hearing and smell are excellent, which help them find the prey more easily.
